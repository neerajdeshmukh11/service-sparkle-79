## Smart Home Services Platform — Build Plan

### Phase 1: Foundation & Auth
- Design system (professional dark/light theme with brand colors)
- Login/Register pages for Admin, Provider, Customer with role selection
- Protected routes & role-based navigation
- Layout components (Sidebar, Header, Breadcrumbs)

### Phase 2: Admin Panel (Stories #1-#24)
- Dashboard with KPIs (revenue, bookings, users, providers)
- User Management (list, search, filter, block/unblock)
- Provider Management (list, onboard, activate/deactivate, verify docs, performance)
- Service Categories & Sub-services management
- Pricing configuration
- Bookings management
- Transactions & fraud detection
- Complaints & refund management
- Notifications management
- Reports & earnings
- Promotions & coupons

### Phase 3: Provider Portal (Stories #25-#44)
- Provider dashboard with earnings overview
- Profile management
- Job notifications & accept/decline
- Assigned jobs & job history
- Availability toggle
- Job stages (started, arrived, completed)
- Image uploads (before/after)
- Extra charges
- Wallet & earnings analytics
- Service categories management
- Ratings & feedback view
- Complaint submission

### Phase 4: Customer Portal (Stories #45-#59)
- Service search & category browsing
- Price estimates
- Service scheduling/booking
- Provider tracking (map placeholder)
- Photo uploads
- In-app chat placeholder
- Stage notifications
- Payment & invoices
- Ratings & feedback
- Rebooking
- AI chatbot placeholder

All using mock data, professional UI with smooth animations.
